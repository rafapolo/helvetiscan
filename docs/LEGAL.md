# Legal & Ethical Framework

HelvetiScan performs passive, non-intrusive reconnaissance of publicly accessible internet infrastructure. All scans are rate-limited and collect only information that is voluntarily exposed by target systems — DNS records, TLS certificates, HTTP response headers, and open port states. No authentication is attempted, no credentials are tested, and no vulnerabilities are exploited. Individual domain results are never published; all public-facing data is aggregated and anonymized. Personal data processing complies with the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (DSG/nDSG). Security issues discovered during scanning are handled through responsible disclosure. For questions regarding scanning methodology or to request exclusion, contact rafael@extrapolo.com.
